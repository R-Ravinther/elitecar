About Us

Our Success Story

Elite Car Rental was created by Mr. Suresh MK in 2012 with just 5 taxis and has grown to 150+ taxis to meet the consumer demand in Taxi Services providing pan India Service. We at Elite Car Rentals are specialized in reasonably priced, quality taxi cab services from locally owned to operated branches across Banglaore Metorpolitan City. We have a strong team of drivers who have fulfilled all statutory requirements like Police Verification, Good Health and come from the most reliable references.

Our vehicles carry everything from small economy rental cars to luxury sedans, as well as minivans, fifteen passenger vans, SUV's and crossovers to rent by the hour, day, week or month. Our customers testimonials speak about our services which makes us the most reliable taxi company who offer a wide variety of services to make your travel around Bangalore or inter City/state travel more comfortable, safer and faster.

Our vast variety of fleets will fulfill your taxi requirement 24/7. You, as our customer have the freedom to choose a vehicle of your convenience and hire cab in Bangalore. Our local pick up and drop service shall overcome the challenge of getting around in Bangalore and you can never worry about the frustrating travel conditions with our taxi service in Bangalore.
